Help

Each course element, like this one, allows you to input grades and weights in pairs, which will instantly update the average displayed at the top, as well as the overall average at the top of the page.

While the grade percentage can be entered as a whole number, a decimal, or a fraction, the weight percentage can only be entered as a whole number.

To add more pairs, simply click the add button at the bottom of the element. To delete a pair, simply click the remove button beside it.
